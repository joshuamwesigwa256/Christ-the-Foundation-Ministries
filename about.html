Our Story

Christ the Rock Foundation Ministries is a christ centered ministries founded in 2004 by our Senior Pastors Pr. James and jacqueline Mwesigwa by the will and purpose of God. It was after they verily heard the voice of God themselves alongside confirmations by a multitude of other ministers of God that they launched.

Our Mission

Our mission is the establish the authority of the Word of God in the lives of men like a house built on the rock.

Our Vision

We long to have pillars in every continent walking in the authority of the Word and the power of God.

Our Leadership

Pastor James Mwesigwa is the Senior Minister and founder of the ministry. He received Jesus as Lord in 1987 from a on a crusade when an Evangelist Lin Linderstome(from Canada) touched Pr. James, a young 21 year old from a muslim back-ground. He graduated at All Nations For Christ Bible Institute in Benin city, Nigeria in 1991 and served as an Associate Pastor at Faith Arena Church Kabowa until God called him to start this ministry in 2004. Pr. James is married to his beautiful wife Jacqueline Mwesigwa and they are blessed with 3 wonderful children--Joshua, Joy and Jessica.

Our Locations

With our base at Kabowa, we are blessed to have over ten campuses all over the country plus one in the UK. We hope to establish more and more strongholds all-over the continent like it is in our vision to the glory of God.
