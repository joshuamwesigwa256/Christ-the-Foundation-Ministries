Our Story

Christ the Rock Foundation Ministries is a christ centered ministries founded in 2004 by our Senior Pastors Pr. James and jacqueline Mwesigwa by the will and purpose of God. It was after they verily heard the voice of God themselves alongside confirmations by a multitude of other ministers of God that they launched.

Our Mission

Our mission is the establish the authority of the Word of God in the lives of men like a house built on the rock.

Our Vision

We long to have pillars in every continent walking in the authority of the Word and the power of God.
